About Me

I am Erin Anderson. I was born on the 28th of March 2010 in Johannesburg, South Africa, and I moved to New Zealand in November 2018. I have one older brother who also attends Botany College; we are 11 months apart in age. I have four cats: 3 Devon Rex's and one Sphynx.

Some of my hobbies include watching movies and TV shows in my free time. I like to play games online with my older brother, for example, Apex Legends, Valorant, Call of Duty, Fortnite, and occasionally Roblox. I love hanging out with my family and friends whenever I have free time. I enjoy shopping at the mall with my friends.

I also play squash and occasionally field hockey. I play squash at the Howick Squash Club; my father, brother, and I all play together weekly. I also train in squash during the weekend, although I don't play professionally.

I love playing with my four cats, and I also love sleeping.
